welcome

Welcome to Seattle Pat's page, home of the iconic Salmon Cookie! Our unique product was inspired by a combination of the sensory delights found in Seattle's own Pike Place Market, where you can enjoy both the tastiest fresh Pacific salmon and the zen of coffeehouse culture.

Packed with all of the health benefits of salmon's super nutrients without any of the fishy taste, our mysteriously perfect cookies are the perfect complement to a warm beverage and rainy day.
